Security

How secure is RELAXed & PouchDB?

  • "Data that can be accessible to unauthenticated users makes an ideal candidate for offline storage."
  • "No role, ACL or ownership checks in CouchDB."
  • "Filtered replication: from Couch to Pouch and back."

Afterthoughts

Is RELAXed+PouchDB enterprise ready?

  • Lack of strong permission control suggests, no.
  • Suitable for publicly available content, without write permissions.

IBM Cloudant Envoy

Is RELAXed+PouchDB preferable over any other option?

  • Tie: Document store in the browser that is available offline.
  • Lose: PouchDB doesn't have per-document controls.
  • Win: PouchDB handles all of the syncing for you.
